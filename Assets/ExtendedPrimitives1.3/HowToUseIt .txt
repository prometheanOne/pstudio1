	How to use it :

- Once you have extract the package in your Unity Editor, a new tab "FormationFacile" will appear.

- By clicking on it, you will see the "Extended Primitives" tab. Click on it.

- A new windows appear in the editor, place it where you want.

- You can create new primitives by clicking on the small image representing the primitive that will be created on the scene as a gameObject (prefab).
